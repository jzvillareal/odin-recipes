Campfire Smore

Ingredients

  • 4 whole graham crackers
  • 4 marshmallows
  • 4 pieces chocolate (such as Hershey's)

Directions

  1. Break all graham crackers in half to create 8 squares. Place one square of chocolate on 4 graham squares.
  2. Place marshmallows on skewer or marshmallow roasting stick. Roast over open flame or grill until toasted to your preference.
  3. Transfer marshmallow onto graham cracker with chocolate, then top with second graham cracker square.
