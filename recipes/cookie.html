Ingredients

  • 1 cup (2 sticks) unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup semisweet chocolate chips

Directions

  1. Begin by creaming together softened butter and granulated sugar until smooth.
  2. Beat in eggs, one at a time, then add vanilla extract for flavor.
  3. Gradually mix in all-purpose flour until a smooth dough forms.
  4. Fold in semisweet chocolate chips until evenly distributed.
  5. Drop spoonfuls of dough onto lined baking sheets and bake at 350°F (175°C) for 8-10 minutes until golden brown. Allow to cool before enjoying your delicious homemade chocolate cookies!
