nutella

Ingredients

  • 2 slices whole wheat bread
  • 2 tablespoons nutella

Directions

  1. Heat griddle.
  2. Lightly spread butter on the outsides of two slices of bread.
  3. Spread Nutella on the inside and sandwich together.
  4. Grill 2 mins per side.
  5. Slice in half.
  6. Serve warm.
